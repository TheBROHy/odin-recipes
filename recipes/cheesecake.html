Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert made with cream cheese, sugar, and eggs. It can be baked or no-bake and is often served with fruit toppings or chocolate sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add sugar and mix well.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in vanilla, flour, and salt.
  5. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  6. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until the center is set. Let it cool completely.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
  8. Top with sour cream and lemon juice mixture if desired.
  9. Slice and serve chilled.
